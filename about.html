Mission Statement:

We aim to provide an inclusive environment to our dancers to nurture their passion for dance. Within the community we want to increase visibility of South Asian culture.

Desi Dance Network:

Desi Dance Network Inc. was officially founded in 2014 to foster a community amongst artists engaged in competitive South Asian dance. DDN Inc. was founded on the principle of community building and re-empowering the South Asian community by reconnecting multigenerational South Asian dancers to their roots.
